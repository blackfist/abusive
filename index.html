Abusive Power
A study of publicly-disclosed incidents of abuse by law enforcement

The police abuse database attempts to collect statistics about clear cases of police abusing their position in ways that violate the civil rights of the public. The information is sourced from publicly-disclosed articles that are reported in the news. The database is limited to instances of police abuse in the United States. The data includes incidents where an officer abuses his or her power while on duty or makes use of police resources when off-duty in a way that violates the civil rights and standards of professionalism the public expects from its police service.
